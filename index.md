## Introduction

Baseball Mechanics ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze baseball pitching, hitting, and catching mechanics. Many of the players analyzed in this App are children, and we treat that as the central fact of this policy rather than a footnote.

This policy explains what we collect, where it is stored, who processes it, and what you can do about it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide at Setup

- **Player name** (required) — personalizes analysis reports.
- **Player age** (required) — determines age-appropriate coaching language and triggers the protections in Section 5. Age is required precisely *because* the protections in Section 5 are impossible to apply to a player whose age we never asked for.
- **Role** (player, coach, or parent).
- **Email address** (optional for players 13 and over; **required** for under-13 accounts, where it must be the parent's or guardian's — see Section 5).
- **Marketing preference** — whether you ticked the optional box to receive product-update emails. It is off unless you turn it on.

### 1.2 Account

The App creates an account for you. If you provide an email address you receive a cloud account (sign-in by one-time code; we never see or store a password). If you do not provide one, the App signs you in **anonymously** — a random identifier with no personal information attached to it.

We do not collect passwords, payment card numbers, government ID numbers, or precise location.

### 1.3 Video Frames You Submit for Analysis

You select a video from your device and the App extracts still frames at specific mechanical positions. **Those still images — not the original video — are sent to our server for AI analysis, and they are not retained after the analysis completes.** We never receive or store the original video file, and there is no library, archive, or training set of user video anywhere in our systems.

**Please do not capture frames containing people other than the player being analyzed.**

### 1.4 Analysis and Session History

Your analysis results (mechanics scores, identified strengths and weaknesses, recommended drills) are stored on your device. If you have a cloud account, they are **also stored on our servers** so they are available across your devices and over time.

### 1.5 Recruiting Profile (optional)

If you choose to create one: position, graduation year, school, state, height, weight, bats/throws, and your mechanics scores. See Section 4 for who can see it. **This feature is switched off entirely for players under 13 and cannot be turned on.**

### 1.6 Team Data (optional)

If a coach creates a team, we store the roster and the members' mechanics scores so the coach can see team-level progress.

### 1.7 Subscription Status

If you purchase a subscription, we store **whether you have an active subscription and which one**. Payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google — **we never see or store your payment details.**

### 1.8 Diagnostic Information

If the App hits a technical error we receive a report containing the error, the screen it happened on, your OS and app version, and your name and email if you provided one. Used only to fix bugs; never sold, and never used for marketing.

### 1.9 What We Do NOT Collect

- Precise or approximate GPS location
- Contacts, call logs, or messages
- Browsing activity outside the App
- Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data
- Biometric identifiers, facial recognition data, or voiceprints
- Payment card or bank account information

## 4. Recruiting Profile Visibility — You Control This

If you create a recruiting profile, **you choose** who can see it:

- **Private** — nobody but you.
- **Coaches only** — the coaches of teams you belong to. This is the maximum for any player under 13.
- **Recruiters** — visible to recruiter accounts in the App's search and discovery tools.
- **Public** — additionally reachable through a shareable link you can send to a college coach who does not use the App. The link contains a long random identifier; it cannot be guessed, but **anyone you send it to can open it**, so share it deliberately.

You can change the visibility or delete the profile at any time. Lowering the visibility immediately stops it being served at the higher level.

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

**Baseball Mechanics is used to analyze children, and we designed this section on the assumption that the player is a minor unless we know otherwise.**

### 5.1 Age is required

We ask for the player's age at setup and it cannot be skipped. If we do not know a player's age, we apply the **stricter** treatment, not the looser one — an unknown age is treated as a child.

### 5.2 How a parent or guardian gives verifiable consent.

If the player is under 13, the account can only be set up by a parent or legal guardian. Setup does not activate the account. Instead:

1. The guardian provides their own email address, which becomes the owner of the account and must be verified by a one-time code we email at sign-in.
2. We then email that address a unique, single-use confirmation link. The account stays locked and cannot be used until the guardian clicks it. Clicking the link is the guardian's verifiable consent, and we record the date and time it occurred.
3. Shortly after, we send a second confirmation email to the same address, which also contains a link to withdraw consent at any time.

This "email-plus" process is a verifiable parental consent method permitted under COPPA because a child's information in this App is never disclosed publicly — every under-13 player is restricted to coach-only visibility (see Section 5.1). Until a guardian completes step 2, no analysis, profile, or other data for that player is used beyond what is strictly necessary to hold the pending account.

### 5.3 Under-13 players are never published

For any player under 13, **recruiter and public visibility are switched off and cannot be turned on.** Their recruiting profile can be shared with the coaches of their own team and no further. It is never listed in recruiter search, never returned by discovery, and never reachable by a public link. This is enforced in the App and independently enforced again on our server, so it cannot be bypassed by a modified client.

### 5.4 Marketing

We do not send marketing email to children. The only address on an under-13 account is the guardian's, and it receives product-update email only if the guardian themselves opted in.

### 5.5 Parental rights

A parent or guardian may review or delete their child's data at any time by emailing **mechanicssupport@gmail.com** from the guardian address on the account. We respond within 30 days. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we delete it promptly.
A guardian may also withdraw consent instantly using the link in the confirmation email, or from within the app's pending-consent screen; doing so locks and then deletes the child's account and data.
