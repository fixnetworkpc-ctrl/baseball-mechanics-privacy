## Introduction

Baseball Mechanics ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze baseball pitching and batting mechanics. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who uses our App, including children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you first open the App, you are asked to provide:

- **Your name** (required) — Used to personalize your analysis reports and results emails.
- **Your email address** (optional) — If provided, your analysis results are automatically emailed to you in a formatted report. If you choose not to provide an email address, the App functions fully without it and no email is sent.

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect passwords, payment information, social security numbers, or government-issued ID numbers.

### 1.2 Video Frames You Submit for Analysis

When you use the App to analyze pitching or batting mechanics, you:

1. Select a video from your device's camera roll.
2. Capture still frames from that video at specific mechanical positions.

Those still frames (not the original video) are extracted on your device as JPEG images and sent to our secure backend server for AI analysis. **We do not receive, store, or retain the original video file.** Only the individual frames you capture are transmitted, and they are used solely to generate your analysis results.

**Important:** Do not submit frames that include faces or identifying information of individuals other than the player being analyzed if you do not have their permission.

### 1.3 Error and Diagnostic Information

If the App encounters a technical error, a diagnostic report is automatically sent to the developer. This report includes:

- A description of the error
- The screen or function where the error occurred
- Your device's operating system version and platform (Android/iOS)
- The App version number
- Your name and email address (if you provided one) so the developer can follow up

This information is used solely to diagnose and fix technical problems. It is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Precise GPS location or location data of any kind
- Device contacts, call logs, or messages
- Browsing history or activity outside the App
- Advertising identifiers or tracking data
- Biometric data, facial recognition data, or voiceprints
- Health records or medical information
- Credit card or financial account information

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:

### 3.1 Anthropic (AI Analysis Provider)

The video frames you capture are sent to **Anthropic, PBC** (anthropic.com) for processing by their Claude AI model. Anthropic processes these frames solely to generate your mechanical analysis and does not retain them for training or any other purpose beyond fulfilling the request. Anthropic's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy available at [https://www.anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

### 3.2 Render (Backend Hosting)

Our backend server is hosted by **Render Services, Inc.** (render.com). Your name, email address (if provided), and captured frames pass through Render's infrastructure when analysis is requested. Render's infrastructure is located in the United States. Render's privacy practices are available at [https://render.com/privacy](https://render.com/privacy).

### 3.3 Google / Gmail (Email Delivery)

If you provide an email address, your analysis results are delivered via **Gmail** using Google's mail servers. Your email address and the content of your results report are transmitted through Google's infrastructure for this purpose only. Google's privacy policy is available at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request. We may also disclose information where we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the developer, our users, or the public.

## 5. Children's Privacy

### 5.1 Who This App Is Designed For

Baseball Mechanics is primarily designed for use by **coaches, parents, and guardians** who analyze the baseball mechanics of players they work with or care for. The App is not directed at children under 13 as its primary audience. Children under 13 may use the App under direct parental or guardian supervision, but the App is not intended to be used independently by children under 13.

**The App does not require users to provide an email address.** Email collection is entirely optional and used solely to deliver analysis results. The App functions fully without an email address being provided.

### 5.2 COPPA Compliance

This App is designed to comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**. Specifically:

- The App **does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13** without verifiable parental consent.
- Email addresses are **optional** and are used only to deliver analysis reports — never for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.
- We do not share, sell, or disclose any information collected through the App to third parties for commercial purposes.
- If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate parental consent, we will delete it promptly upon notification.

### 5.3 Parental Consent for Email Collection

If a child under the age of 13 will have analysis results emailed, a parent or legal guardian must provide the email address on the child's behalf. By entering an email address for a child under 13, you represent that you are the parent or legal guardian and consent to our use of that email address as described in this policy.

### 5.4 Parental Rights

If you believe your child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to the App without your consent, or if you wish to review or delete any such information, please contact us at **BaseballMSupport@gmail.com**. We will respond to such requests within 30 days and take appropriate action.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Specifically:

- All data transmitted between the App and our backend server uses encrypted HTTPS connections.
- Anthropic API keys and email credentials are stored securely as environment variables on our server and are never exposed in the App code or transmitted to users.
- Captured video frames are transmitted only for the purpose of analysis and are not stored in any database after processing.

However, no method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Deletion

Because we do not maintain a central database of your personal information, most data associated with your use of the App exists only on your device. You can:

- **Delete all locally stored data** by uninstalling the App from your device.
- **Opt out of email results** by leaving the email field blank during onboarding or by not providing an email address.
- **Request deletion of error reports** by emailing us at fixnetworkpc@gmail.com with your name and approximate date of use.

### 7.2 Opt-Out of Error Reporting

Error reports are sent automatically when the App encounters a crash. To prevent this, you may choose not to use the App. There is no in-app toggle for error reporting in the current version.

### 7.3 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at fixnetworkpc@gmail.com.

### 7.4 EEA / UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the GDPR including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Our legal basis for processing is:

- **Consent:** for email delivery of results (you choose to provide your email)
- **Legitimate interest:** for error reporting to maintain app stability

To exercise your rights or to lodge a complaint, contact us at fixnetworkpc@gmail.com.
